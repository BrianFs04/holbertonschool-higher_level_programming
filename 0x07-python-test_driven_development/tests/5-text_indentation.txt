===========================================
	How to Use 5-text_indentation.py
===========================================

Importing function from the module:
    >>> text_indentation = __import__('5-text_indentation').text_indentation

Now use it:
    >>> text_indentation("Hello. What's your name?")
    Hello.
    <BLANKLINE>
    What's your name?
    <BLANKLINE>

    >>> text_indentation("Hi. Brian")
    Hi.
    <BLANKLINE>
    Brian

    >>>text_indentation(".?:")
    .
    <BLANKLINE>
    ?
    <BLANKLINE>
    :
    <BLANKLINE>

    >>> text_indentation("")
